Chinese Spicy Cucumber Salad

Description

Chinese spicy cucumber salad is a tantalizing and refreshing dish that combines the coolness of crisp cucumbers with a burst of fiery flavors. This popular Sichuan-style salad is a delightful balance of textures and tastes, making it a perfect addition to any meal or a satisfying snack on its own. Thinly sliced cucumbers are marinated in a zesty dressing, typically made from a blend of soy sauce, vinegar, sesame oil, garlic, ginger, and a generous amount of chili oil.

The cucumbers absorb the vibrant flavors of the dressing, creating a harmonious blend of tangy, spicy, and slightly sweet notes. The salad is often garnished with toasted sesame seeds or chopped peanuts for an added crunch and nuttiness. The combination of the cooling cucumbers and the fiery dressing creates a mouthwatering sensation that is both refreshing and invigorating. Chinese spicy cucumber salad is a beloved dish that showcases the dynamic flavors of Sichuan cuisine and is sure to leave your taste buds craving for more.

Ingredients

  • 1 Fresh English Cucumber
  • 3 Garlic Cloves
  • 1/4 Teaspoon Sugar
  • Salt to Taste
  • 1 Tablespoon Sesame Oil
  • 1 Tablespoon Light Soy Sauce
  • 1 Teaspoon Black Vinegar
  • Chili Oil to Taste

Steps

  1. Wash and peel the cucumber.
  2. Crush the cucumber with a knife.
  3. Cut the cucumber into bite-sized pieces.
  4. Transfer the crushed cucumber into a bowl.
  5. Mix with the mashed garlic, salt, and sugar.
  6. Add light soy sauce, vinegar, and chili oil.
  7. Mix again and enjoy!
